About Us

Crafting Quality Garments with Passion

Who We Are

We are a dynamic garment manufacturing firm with over 25 years of experience. Specializing in hosiery, we bring a blend of quality, service, and creativity to the fashion industry.

What Sets Us Apart

Our relentless focus on quality, commitment to service, reasonable pricing, and timely delivery make us a trusted partner in the world of fashion. We don't just manufacture garments; we craft experiences.

Our Labels

We operate with two distinct in-house labels to cater to varying client needs: Sage, our dedicated solution for uniform orders, streamlining production for non-customized items ordered in bulk; and SD, specially designed for products like sports jerseys, where each item is customized with individual names and numbers, ensuring a personalized touch for every customer.

Our Products

From comfortable T-shirts to stylish jackets, we manufacture a diverse range of garments. Our fabrics include cotton, poly-cotton, polyester, looperknit, interlock, supima, spun lycra, and more. Each piece is tailored and printed according to your unique requirements.
